Beta Courses

How it Works?

Create your course, add lessons, content, and whatever else will make your course better.

After your done, submit your course for approval. Once approved, your course will now be accessible to the world, with a community of empowered developers constantly adjusting and improving your course till perfection.

After the initial beta phase, which takes roughly 2-3 weeks after submission, your course will be offered to everyone. Over the course of time, if you recieve high rankings based on courses you've created, you'll earn rewards and prizes.

Why Contribute?

So now you're at a pretty good point. You've mastered the basics and even have a solid grasp of some of the complexities of programming, but more importantly, you want to share that knowledge with the world.

Despite what stuffy professors may tell you, we believe that the best teachers are the most recent. It's near impossible to teach someone something if you can't even remeber a time when you didin't know it! New learners make the best teachers because they recognize specific struggles and hurdles that educators with decades of experience often overlook.
