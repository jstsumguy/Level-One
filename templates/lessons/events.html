About

With collective power of a huge community of active learners and developers, the LevelUp 2015 Fall Hackathon, is a joint venture designed to insipre and invigorate aspiring developers and learns alike to create something incredible.

Collaborate

Hackathons are the perfect place to learn something new. First register either as a team, or an individual. Individuals will be assigned to other individuals.

Design

Once the teams have been formed, your team will be given a choice of exciting projects to work on. You and your team are allowed to use any technology you want but it must be completed within 12 hours. Hackathons are what inspire next gen tech and developers to think creatively in a think-tank of like-minded people.

Develop

Work around the clock, developing with your team, fueled by pure adrenaline, competing against other teams in a battle to the death. This is what hackathons are all about. See rankings live as they happen, and get live collaboration throughout the entire event.
